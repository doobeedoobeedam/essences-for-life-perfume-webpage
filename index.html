Essences for Life was founded in 1986 and is a privately held company. Over the years it has maintained its focus: entire dedication to be one of the most reliable and innovative sources within our prime field of expertise: NATURAL AROMA INGREDIENTS for the fragrance compounding industry worldwide.

By strategic investments in R&D of both novel natural ingredients and new manufacturing processes, we constantly strive to expand the use of our products in existing and new applications as well as markets.

Essences for Life is an ISO 9001 certified company since 2006, and ISO 22000 and FSSC certified since 2014.
